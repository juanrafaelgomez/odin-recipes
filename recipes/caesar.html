Cesar Salad

Home

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Crisp Croutons
  • Salad dressing (either homemade or commercial)

Steps

  1. Rinse the lettuce in cold water.
  2. Spin dry the lettuce leaves. Remove as much water as possible.
  3. Chill lettuce before use.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, add your prepared romaine lettuce, top with croutons and parmesan.
  5. Just before serving, drizzle with your dressing, and toss gently to coat the lettuce and croutons.
