Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground Beef
  • Chopped Tomato
  • Cilantro
  • Lettuce
  • Onion slices
  • Jalapeno Pepper (finely chopped)
  • Limes

Steps

  1. Heat the tortillas just to be tender.
  2. Cook the ground beef, add salt to taste.
  3. Put the cooked beef on the tortilla.
  4. Add some onion, cilantro and the jalapeno.
  5. Dress everything with some lime juice
