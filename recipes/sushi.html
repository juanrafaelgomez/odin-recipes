: Surimi Sushi

Ingredients

  • Seaweed
  • Mayonaisse
  • Sushi Rice
  • Surimi sticks
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Wash and cook the rice for 20 minutes.
  2. When it's still warm, add a table spoon of rice vinegar and two tablespoons of sugar. Mix.
  3. Lightly a Seaweed sheet.
  4. Add a mix of mayo and surimi to one side of the sheet.
  5. Roll it and slice it on equal parts.
